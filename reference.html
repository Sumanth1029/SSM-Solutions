KRC Group is a building materials company that provides high quality products and reliable service to customers and communities throughout the Karnataka,We produce, distribute, and sell ready materials, and we maintain trade relationships in entire Karnataka State. We would appreciate to introduce our self as one of the manufacturer of “Ready Mix Concrete” the state of the art technology in producing the design mix concrete. We have fully computerized and automated Batching plants from the reputed manufactures of Batching plant all over the world.We own the crusher of 200 TPH VSI Crusher with washing unit for Manufacture Sand to produce the quality aggregates required from mechanically operated and automated crusher. We have sound, experienced,qualified & well trained team of Mechanical,operators for transit mixers, Pumps and team of fitters for pipeline fixing etc,. Karnataka Ready Con Mix Concrete is manufactured at state -of-the-art computerized automatic batching & mixing plants with contemporary technology.
